SEO

Just as the name implies, search engine optimazation is a process of developing web content with the search engine in mind. The goal of search engine optimization is to make it easy for search engines to find web contents and present it to the users when they are searching.

Basically it involves making incremental improvements that will enhance user experience and performance.

what you will learn

  • The concept of organic search
  • The difference between between on-page SEO and on-site SEO
  • The anatomy of a search result
  • The concept of crawling, indexing and ranking

ONLINE ADVERTISING

Google Ads is a very powerful online advertising tool that helps businesses connect with potential and existing customers when it matters most. With Google Ads, businesses can target audience based on location, demographics and even interests.

Google Ads also make it possible to control advertersing cost, track and improve performance.
